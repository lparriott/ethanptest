About Us

Second-Hand Sticks is a new and up coming store that specializes in the selling of reused golf clubs. The game of golf continues to expand and in order to keep up with your competition, having the right equipment is vital to success.

Whether you're a weekend warrior, or someone looking to take your game to the next level, we have all the equipment for your needs. While carrying most household brands, we do specialize in Titleist, Taylormade, and Callaway.
